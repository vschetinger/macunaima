About Macunaíma

Macunaíma is a character from Brazilian literature, known for his cunning, laziness, and cleverness. He embodies many aspects of our project: like Macunaíma, our generative models are capable of producing a wide variety of stories and narratives, and like his parrot, they store and recombine ideas to create something new.

Our Project

We aim to harness the power of generative models to create audiovisual narratives. Our system, named after the mythical Macunaíma, strives to generate compelling and diverse narratives in a way that's as unpredictable and creative as its namesake.
